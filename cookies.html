Cookies Policy

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us enhance your experience, analyze site performance, and deliver personalized content about our perfumes and services.

2. How We Use Cookies

UD Fragrance uses cookies to:

  • Track user interactions with our product pages (e.g., views of Eternal Bloom or Green Safaris).
  • Analyze preferences for custom fragrance consultations and scent subscriptions.
  • Improve website functionality, such as remembering your navigation preferences.
  • Support social media integrations (e.g., Facebook, Instagram sharing).

3. Types of Cookies

We use the following cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for website functionality, such as navigating between our Services and Contact pages.
  • Analytics Cookies: Collect data on how users interact with our fragrance product listings and gallery.
  • Third-Party Cookies: Set by social media platforms (e.g., Facebook, Instagram) for sharing content.
