Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

UD Fragrance is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website or engage with our perfume-related services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect:

  • Contact Information: Name, email, phone number, and address provided during fragrance consultations, scent subscriptions, or inquiries via social media (e.g., WhatsApp, Instagram).
  • Preference Data: Fragrance preferences (e.g., floral, woody notes) shared during custom fragrance sessions or workshops.
  • Usage Data: Information on how you interact with our website, such as viewed products (e.g., Lunar Veil, Starlit Breeze) and navigation patterns, collected via cookies.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Process perfume orders and deliver scent subscriptions.
  • Personalize fragrance consultations and workshop experiences.
  • Improve our website and marketing, such as promoting Green Safaris or Eternal Bloom.
  • Respond to inquiries via email (anikpefidelis21@gmail.com) or WhatsApp (+234 9012557203).

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with third-party services (e.g., payment processors, social media platforms) solely to facilitate perfume orders or marketing campaigns, in compliance with Nigerian data protection laws.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable measures to protect your data, such as secure storage of consultation records. However, no online transmission is completely secure, and you share information at your own risk.

6. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us at anikpefidelis21@gmail.com. You can also opt out of marketing communications or manage cookies via your browser settings.

7. Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at anikpefidelis21@gmail.com or +234 9012557203.
